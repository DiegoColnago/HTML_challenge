Ethereum

Ethereum is a global, open-source platform for decentralized applications.

Ethereum is an open source, public, blockchain-based distributed computing platform and operating system featuring smart contract functionality.

Ether is the cryptocurrency generated by the Ethereum platform as a reward to mining nodes for computations performed and is the only currency accepted in the payment of transaction fees.

Launched in 2015, Ethereum is the world’s programmable blockchain.

This is Ethereum

Ethereum is creating an internet:

  • Where money and payments are built in;
  • Where users can own their data, and your apps don’t spy and steal from you;
  • Where everyone has access to an open financial system;
  • Built on neutral, open-access infrastructure, controlled by no company or person.
